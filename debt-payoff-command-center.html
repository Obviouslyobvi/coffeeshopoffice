Frequently Asked Questions

What file format are these PDFs? Can I edit them? ▼
All 8 files are in PDF format. You can print them as-is, or fill them digitally using any PDF reader (Adobe Acrobat, Preview on Mac, or any free PDF app). Form fields are editable so you can fill in your numbers digitally and save your progress.
Do I need specific software to use these? ▼
No. Just a PDF reader—which every computer, tablet, and phone has built-in. You don't need Excel, Word, or any special software. Works on Windows, Mac, iPad, Android, or any device.
How long does the system cover? ▼
The Monthly Payment Tracker covers 36 months (3 years), which is realistic for most debt payoff timelines. The other tools (inventory, strategy planner, debt-free calculator) work with any timeline—if your payoff is longer or shorter, adjust the numbers and it works perfectly.
Will this system work for my specific debts? ▼
Yes. The system works with any type of debt: credit cards, student loans, personal loans, medical debt, car loans, or a mix. The inventory, tracker, and letter templates are debt-agnostic. The strategy planner applies to any payoff approach (Snowball, Avalanche, or hybrid).
Is this system for couples or individuals? ▼
Both. Individual users love the accountability and structure. Couples love that the system creates a shared plan and shared milestones—it reduces conflict by making payoff collaborative. Print two copies if you want separate trackers, or share one and fill it together.
Can I use this with my spouse? ▼
Absolutely. Many couples buy the system and use it together. Fill out the inventory as a team, discuss the strategy planner options together, set a shared debt-free date, and track progress together. The shared accountability often keeps couples more motivated.
What if my payoff takes longer or shorter than 3 years? ▼
The 3-year tracker fits most cases. If your payoff is shorter, you'll finish before the tracker ends (which is great!). If longer, the tracker shows your pattern and you can extend it using the same monthly format. The Debt-Free Date Calculator helps you figure out the real timeline upfront.
When will I get my files? ▼
Immediately after purchase. You'll receive an email with a download link. Download and save the files to your computer or access them in your LemonSqueezy library anytime. The files are yours forever—no subscription, no expiration.
Can I print these or must I use them digitally? ▼
Both. Print them if you prefer paper, use them digitally if that's easier, or do both—some people print the tracker to post on the fridge for daily motivation, and use the spreadsheet version on their computer. It's your system; use it however works best.
