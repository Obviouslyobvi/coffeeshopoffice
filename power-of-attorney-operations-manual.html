Frequently Asked Questions

Can I edit the PDFs?

Yes, all PDFs are provided in an editable format. You can fill them digitally before printing, or print and hand-fill them.

What if I need to manage multiple people's affairs?

This manual works for any single person. You can print additional copies for each principal you manage.

Do I need any special software?

No. PDFs open in any standard PDF reader (Adobe, Preview, web browsers). No special software required.

Can I share this with family members and advisors?

Absolutely. The manual is designed for sharing relevant sections with family members, attorneys, accountants, and doctors.

Is this legal advice?

No. This is an organizational tool. Always consult with your attorney about specific legal requirements in your state.

What if I'm not appointed yet but want to prepare?

Perfect use case. Having this system ready means you can implement immediately if appointed, or help someone else who needs it.
