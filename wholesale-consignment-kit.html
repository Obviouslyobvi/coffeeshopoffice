This is a template for reference and guidance only. It's not a substitute for legal advice. For high-value partnerships or specific legal concerns, consult a lawyer in your jurisdiction.

How many stores can I track with these templates?

The Retailer Outreach Tracker has space for 20+ retailers, and the Consignment Inventory Log has space for 25+ product entries across multiple locations. You can print and duplicate pages or convert to your own spreadsheet as you grow.

Absolutely. This kit is designed for makers who are new to wholesale and consignment. The templates are straightforward, the pricing calculator walks you through the math, and the outreach tracker helps you organize your efforts.

Can I use these for international retailers?

Yes! The templates are universal. You can adapt them for any currency, shipping method, and business arrangement. Currency symbols and payment terms can be customized in the templates.
