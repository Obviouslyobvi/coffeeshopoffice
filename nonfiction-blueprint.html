The 4 Phases

Phase 1: Foundation

Days 1–2

Phase 2: Writing

Days 3–13

Write every chapter using your outline as a roadmap. Start with whatever interests you most. Explain each subtopic in your own words. Keep moving.

Phase 3: Editing

Days 14–20

Research day. First draft assembly. Read-through. Four days of revision and polish. Color-coded draft system keeps you organized.

Phase 4: Finalize

Day 21

Final corrections. Spell-check. Comprehensive checklist. Your manuscript is complete. Then take a 3-day break before your final review.
