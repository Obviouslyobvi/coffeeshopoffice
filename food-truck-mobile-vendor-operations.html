Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need special software to use these templates?

No. All PDFs work with any PDF reader. Fillable versions work on any device with a PDF app. GoodNotes version is optimized for iPad + stylus, but works on any tablet or computer.

Can I print these templates?

Yes. All PDFs are designed to print clearly on Letter, A4, A5, or Half-Letter sizes. Print once and organize in a binder, or print as needed for digital planning.

Are these templates fillable?

Five of the templates are interactive fillable PDFs (Daily Planner, Commissary Tracker, Inventory, Location Scouting, Maintenance Log). You can fill them on your tablet or computer. The other five are printable-only templates.

How long is each PDF?

Varies by template: Getting Started (2 pages), Daily Operations (4), Menu Costing (3), Commissary (2), Event Center (3), Compliance (3), Inventory (3), Location Scouting (2), Financial (3), Maintenance (2). Total: 27 pages.

Can I modify the templates?

Yes. Download the PDFs, fill them with your information, and save them as your working copies. Templates are yours to customize for your business.

What if I'm not sure this is right for me?
