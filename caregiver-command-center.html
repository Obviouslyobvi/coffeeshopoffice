    Stop Drowning in Details

    Managing your aging parent's care is overwhelming—not because you don't care, but because the logistics are scattered everywhere. Medical information in your notes. Appointments on multiple calendars. Insurance details you can never find. Medications you're terrified of forgetting.

    The real problem: You're holding all of this in your head while simultaneously working, managing your own family, and trying to take care of yourself. That's not sustainable.

    The solution: A simple, comprehensive, printable system designed specifically for family caregivers. Everything you need in one place. Nothing you don't.

    What's Inside (10 Worksheets)

    Each worksheet tackles a specific part of parent care, so nothing falls through the cracks.

    Medical

    Medical Info Master

    One-page summary of your parent's health conditions, surgeries, allergies, current medications, and healthcare providers. Take it to every appointment.

    Medical

    Medication Management Tracker

    Log all medications, dosages, frequencies, side effects, pharmacy info, and refill dates. No more "is it once or twice a day?" confusion.

    Appointments

    Appointment Tracker

    Organize all healthcare visits, tests, procedures, and follow-ups. Never miss a detail or overlook follow-up care.

    Scheduling

    Caregiver Schedule Coordinator

    Master schedule for who's providing care when. Essential when multiple family members are sharing caregiver responsibilities.

    Daily Care

    Daily Care Log

    Record daily health observations, mood changes, appetite, sleep quality, and concerns. Helps you spot patterns and report accurately to healthcare providers.

    Contacts

    Important Contacts Sheet

    Master list of all doctors, pharmacies, emergency contacts, insurance companies, and family members. Print it, post it, share it.

    Safety

    Home Safety Assessment

    Systematic checklist for identifying fall risks, hazards, accessibility issues, and safety modifications in your parent's home.

    Legal & Financial

    Legal & Financial Checklist

    Guided checklist for gathering critical documents—Power of Attorney, living will, insurance policies, financial accounts, property info. Reduces anxiety about what you might be missing.

    Transitions

    Care Transition Planner

    Detailed planning tool for moves to new care settings—assisted living, nursing care, or home care. Nothing falls through the cracks during transitions.

    Self-Care

    Respite & Self-Care Tracker

    Track respite care dates, your own health appointments, and self-care commitments. A reminder that your wellbeing matters too.
