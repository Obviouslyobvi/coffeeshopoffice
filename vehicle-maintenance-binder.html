Vehicle Maintenance Binder

The complete printable system to track maintenance, prevent expensive repairs, and take control of your vehicle care

You skip the oil change by two weeks. Then by three months.

Suddenly it's been 6 months since your last service. You have no idea what maintenance your car actually needs, when the tires were last rotated, or if your registration is about to expire.

Then you find a weird noise, take it to the mechanic, and get hit with a $300 bill for something that should have been $50 in preventative care.

Vehicle maintenance isn't supposed to be a guessing game. This 8-PDF system changes that.

What's Inside: 8 Printable PDFs

Vehicle Information Sheets (3 pages)

Complete info for up to 3 vehicles: year, make, model, VIN, insurance details, dealer and mechanic contacts. Everything about your car in one place, so you're never searching for a contact number during an emergency.

Maintenance Schedule (2 pages)

Mileage-based AND time-based maintenance intervals for oil changes, tire rotation, brakes, filters, fluids and more. Check it monthly so nothing falls through the cracks and you catch small issues before they become expensive repairs.

Service History Log (2 pages)

Date, mileage, service performed, shop, cost, and notes. Every service in chronological order. Prove your maintenance history when selling your car, and never wonder "when was that service done?"

Tire & Brake Tracker (1 page)

Dedicated tracking for tires and brakes: purchase dates, tread depth, rotation dates, pad replacement, and condition notes. Know your tire and brake status at a glance.

Fuel & Mileage Log (2 pages)

Record each fill-up: date, odometer, gallons, cost per gallon, and calculated MPG. Track fuel efficiency over time and spot when something's wrong before it becomes an expensive problem.

Insurance & Registration Tracker (1 page)

Policy numbers, renewal dates, registration expiration, emissions tests, safety inspections. Never miss a deadline again, and always know when your registration or insurance is expiring.

Repair Cost Tracker (2 pages)

Annual costs by vehicle, costs by category (engine, brakes, electrical, etc.), and a detailed log of every repair. Know exactly how much you're spending on vehicle maintenance and catch repair patterns.

Getting Started Guide (2 pages)

Setup instructions, printing tips, guidance on maintenance intervals, and quick-start advice. Everything you need to start using your binder today.
