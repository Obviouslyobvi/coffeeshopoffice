Frequently Asked Questions

What format do I receive the files in? +

All files are in PDF format. You'll receive 8 separate PDFs (14 pages total) that you can download immediately after purchase. PDFs are editable and printable, so you can fill them in digitally on your computer or tablet, or print them and fill in by hand.

Can I print these and use them in my garden? +

Absolutely! These are designed to be used on screen or on paper. Many gardeners print the tracking sheets and keep them in a garden notebook or binder. The PDFs are fully printable and optimized to look great on paper.

Do I need any special software to open them? +

No. PDFs open with any free PDF reader (Adobe Reader, Preview on Mac, built-in PDF viewers on phones and tablets). No special software required. If you want to edit them digitally, you can use any PDF editor or even many word processors.

Will these work for my growing zone? +

Yes. The Garden Planner includes guidance for adjusting planting dates based on your frost dates and growing zone. You'll fill in your specific zone information and use that to plan your seasonal timeline. Works for USDA zones 2-11.

How many plants/seeds can I track? +

The Plant Care Log tracks up to 15 individual plants with detailed notes. The Seed Inventory Tracker handles 40+ seed varieties. The system is designed for home gardeners managing single or multiple beds. If you have a very large operation, you might print and repeat the tracking pages.

Is this for vegetable gardening only? +

No. While the system emphasizes vegetables for maximum yield, it includes sections for herbs and flowers. You can use it for any combination of vegetables, herbs, flowers, and ornamental plants. The tracking sheets work for any plant type.

Can I use this system year after year? +

Yes! The system is designed as an evergreen resource. You buy it once and have it forever. Each year you can reuse the planners and create new tracking sheets, or save your digital files and start fresh annually. Many gardeners use it year after year to build a complete growing history.

What if I'm a complete beginner? +

Perfect! This system is designed with beginners in mind. The Garden Planner walks you through everything step-by-step, including what to plant when based on your zone. All the PDFs have clear instructions and are beginner-friendly. Many of our users bought this for their first garden and had great success.
