The Method

From one to one thousand in three days. A book by day five.

1

Day One: The Seed Ideas

Write 10 ideas that could each become the foundation for an entire book. Broad concepts from your expertise. Takes about 20 minutes.

Output: 10 ideas
2

Day Two: The First Expansion

For each of your 10 seed ideas, generate 10 sub-ideas. Chapter concepts, key arguments, stories, frameworks, case studies. Work through one seed at a time.

Output: 100 ideas
3

Day Three: The Full Expansion

Each of your 100 sub-ideas gets 10 supporting details. Specific examples, data points, anecdotes, action steps, lessons learned. This is your book content.

Output: 1,000 ideas
4-5

Days Four and Five: Fill in the Blanks

Connect your ideas into continuous narrative. Write transitions, introductions, and conclusions. Shape the hierarchical structure into a manuscript that reads like a book.

Output: Complete first draft
