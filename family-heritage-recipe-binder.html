Frequently Asked Questions

What file format do I receive? +
You receive a set of print-ready PDF files immediately after purchase. Each PDF is optimized for standard home or office printing. No special software needed—just open with any PDF reader and print on your preferred paper type.
How many recipes can I include? +
The recipe index accommodates 50+ recipes, but you're not limited to that number. The system is modular—use what you need and print additional templates as your collection grows. Many families use multiple binders for different branches or traditions.
Can I print this in black & white? +
Yes! The design is optimized for color printing (the burgundy and cream palette is beautiful in color), but it works perfectly well in black and white if you prefer. The layout and readability remain excellent either way.
Can I customize the pages? +
The PDFs are print-ready and designed to be filled in by hand. You can add photos, handwritten notes, family tree details, or use a marker to highlight sections. Some families also scan their printed binder and add digital notes or photos. The modular design means you only use what fits your family.
How do I bind the pages together? +
The pages work with any standard binding method: 3-hole punch and brads, spiral binding, comb binding, or traditional binder rings. Some families simply use a folder or keep them loose. The 19 pages are designed to fit standard binder layouts. Choose whatever feels right for your family.
Is there a physical/printed version available? +
This product is a printable PDF download. You print it yourself at home or at a local print shop (like FedEx Office or Staples). We recommend printing on cardstock or quality paper for durability. This approach gives you control over binding, customization, and cost.
When do I get access? +
Immediately after purchase! You'll receive a download link via email within minutes. The PDF files are yours to download and save forever. No expiration date, no subscription required.
Can I print multiple copies for my family? +
Yes! For personal use with your immediate family, you can print as many copies as you need. Each family member can have their own binder to fill in. For larger commercial distribution or professional use, please contact us about a business license.
What if I have printing questions? +
Our customer support team is here to help! We can answer questions about printing, paper recommendations, binding options, or customization ideas. Reach out to support@homesteadplanning.co with any questions.
