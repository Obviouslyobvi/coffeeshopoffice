How it works

Step 1: Set your Prime Objective. One outcome. Not three. One.

Step 2: Run the Pre-Flight Checklist. Phone gone. Tabs closed. DND on. Water ready.

Step 3: Work in three phases. Minutes 0-30: push through initial resistance. Minutes 31-60: this is your flow zone. Minutes 61-90: final push and polish.

Step 4: Capture distracting thoughts in the Attention Leak Log. Don't act on them. Just write them down and keep working.

Step 5: Debrief. Did you hit your objective? What worked? What changes next time?

That's one session. Do it 5 times a week. Watch what happens to your output.
