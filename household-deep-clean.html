What format are the files?
All 8 files are PDFs designed for US Letter (8.5" x 11") printing. They open in any PDF reader and print on any standard printer.
Can I print multiple copies?
Yes. Print as many copies as you need for personal use. Print fresh checklists each season, give copies to family members, whatever works for you.
Is this a physical product?
No. This is an instant digital download. You'll receive the PDF files immediately after purchase. Nothing is shipped.
Do I need to clean my whole house at once?
No! The system is designed to be used one room or one zone at a time. Work at your own pace. The zone planner helps you spread deep cleans across 5 weeks.
How is this different from the Home Maintenance Planner?
The Home Maintenance Planner covers repairs, seasonal maintenance, and home upkeep (furnace, gutters, appliances). This product covers cleaning and decluttering. They're complementary - one keeps your home maintained, the other keeps it clean.
Can I use this on a tablet instead of printing?
Yes. The PDFs work on tablets and phones too. Some people prefer to print the checklists and use a pen, but digital use works fine.
