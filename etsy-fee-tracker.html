The Problem with "Profitable" Etsy Shops

You sell a handmade candle for $24. Sounds profitable, right? But then Etsy takes its cut:

$0.25 processing fee (3% + $0.25)
$9.99 shipping label
$9.99 materials
$9.99 labor
$9.99 packaging

That $24 sale? You kept $9.99. Your margin is 26%.

If you don't track this, you don't know this. And you can't fix what you can't see.
